General Questions

CreatiLink is a platform that connects customers with creative professionals. Whether you need graphic design, video editing, photography, or videography services, we help you find and hire the perfect creator for your project.

Simply sign up, choose whether you're a customer or creator, complete your profile, and you're ready to go! Customers can begin posting projects, and creators can start browsing and applying to available projects.

For Customers

After logging in, click "Post Project" in the navigation bar. Fill in project details including title, description, category, budget, and deadline. Creators will then apply to your project with their quotes.
Review applications from creators, check their profiles, portfolios, and ratings. Compare quotes and timelines, then assign the creator that best fits your needs.
We use Stripe for secure payments. Pay when you accept a creator's quote. The creator receives payment after the project is marked as complete. We charge a 10% platform fee.
Communicate with your creator about any concerns. Most creators offer revisions. If issues persist, contact our support team for assistance.

For Creators

Browse available projects in the "Browse Projects" section. Filter by category, budget, or search keywords to find projects that match your skills.
Click on a project, read the requirements, and submit your application with your quote, timeline, and a personalized message explaining why you're the best fit.
Payment is released after the customer marks the project as complete. Funds are transferred to your account minus the 10% platform fee.
Complete your profile with a professional bio, list your skills, upload portfolio samples, and complete projects successfully to earn reviews and build your reputation.
